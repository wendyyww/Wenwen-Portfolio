Health Crusaders

Designing a Health App from Scratch

Problem Statement:

We are concerned about the products sold through social media platform these days, especially for health products. It is hard to regulate product safety issues through these purchases because everyone nowadays can own a business on social media. It is even harder for people to check these products' credibilities. Also, influencers who promote health products may lie about effects. We aim to find a way to promote health product sales and provide customers with credible suggestions.
