Designing a Health App from Scratch

Problem Statement:

We are concerned about the products sold through social media platform these days, especially for health products. It is hard to regulate product safety issues through these purchases because everyone nowadays can own a business on social media. It is even harder for people to check these products' credibilities. Also, influencers who promote health products may lie about effects. We aim to find a way to promote health product sales and provide customers with credible suggestions.

Defining a practical problem statement and identifying user group through interview analysis was the crucial first steps that we undertook. We tried to interview a diverse group of potential users consist of interviewees from multi-aged and multi-gender groups.

Personas

After user research, we designed personas for our target user group to better locate main functions we need to include in our App. We had 6 interviews with people from a variety of age and gender group, and people from different job roles. We designed the above personas based on these detailed interviews. To cover a wide range of users, therefore we created persona 1, who is a working mom and wants to save time on researching products, and persona 2, an old, stubborn male who wants to gather all necessary information for products at ease to cope with his picky wife.

Storyboard

This story, created by me, delineates the story of the old man using our app. He is stubborn and lazy, and he makes purchase decision at once. Because of this, he had to fight with his wife, who cares about her husband's health a lot and who is extra alert of products sold online, very often. However, with the help of our app, he does not have to do product research about ingredients nor he needs to self look-up for product reviews. Ratings from both the consumer and the professional side is provided by us. Finally, he did not waste any time and did not suffer from waiting for approval. He is happy to show his wife all the information together at once, and he got to purchase his desired product.
