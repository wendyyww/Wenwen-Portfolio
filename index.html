About Me!

Jin Wen

UX Designer, Researcher, and Communicator

I am a UX designer + researcher based in Ann Arbor, Michigan. I completed my bachelor's degree in Statistics and Psychology at UCLA. I am now pursuing my master of science in human computer interaction at University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My previous experience was diverse, as I was once a digital marketing director at a education consulting agency, product manager at a tech startup, and a strategy consultant at Bosch automotive. These experience made me grow and made me think about my true passion. Then I discovered UX design, where all my interests align, and here I am.

Outside from the professional life, you may want to know something about myself. I am a professional badminton player, and I just won the runner-up metal in women's single at UM Open Badminton 2022 Fall. I am also a proud mom of 5 dogs and 3 cats at home in Suzhou, China. With my love and passion for animals, I volunteered at the organization for stray animals in Suzhou for more than 7 years. I am thinking about building a website for that organization. I am a believer of how passion and love can change the world - although this sentence sounds silly (lol) but it inspires me every day.
