Client Project with NPower

Redesigning Participant Tracking System

  • Overview
  • Problem
  • Procedure
  • Research
  • Interview

Client Overview

NPower is a nonprofit tech-education organization whose mission is to boost diversity in the tech industry by supporting underrepresented people from underserved communities to launch digital careers. Their target audience is mainly young adults living in poverty, but also includes veterans and their spouses. They offer free, intensive technical training for their students which helps them establish technical-related careers.

The Problem

NPower Michigan currently uses several different tools to track and store their participants' information, including Excel and Salesforce. And our client is facing several problem with their current tracking system. With the goal of expanding the size of their trainee cohorts, NPower needs a better participant tracking system (PTS). They hope to find an active data management system which can store all the participant information in one place, with the ability to capture all data points from the participants' application phases to alumni hoods.

Procedure

As a consulting team, we first decided each team member's roles. I act as the primary client contact and secondary graphic designer for NPower. Our main checkpoints include:
  • Background Research
  • Interviews and observations with clients.
  • Post-interview interpretation
  • Affinity wall.
  • Presentation and final report

Competitor Analysis

Doing background research on an organization often provides insights that can inform inform and supplement the ground-level view of our client's specific problem gained through interviews and observations. To better understand our client, I carried out a ~4000 word background research report in which I synthesized background research about NPower's competitors.

Interview with NPower's Employees

Conducting Interviews is the secret to knowing the problem our client is facing and detecting their pain point.
